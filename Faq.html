Frequently Asked Questions

What types of plywood do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of plywood including marine grade, commercial grade, decorative, and hardwood plywood. Our selection includes various thicknesses and wood types to suit different applications, from furniture making to construction projects.

What is the standard size of your plywood sheets?

Our standard plywood sheets come in 8x4 feet dimensions. However, we understand that projects may require different specifications, so we also offer custom sizing options to meet your specific requirements. Contact our team for custom size inquiries.

Do you provide delivery services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive delivery services across the country. Our delivery network ensures safe and timely delivery of your orders. We also provide tracking information so you can monitor your delivery status in real-time. Bulk orders may qualify for special delivery rates.

How can I place an order?

Ordering is simple! You can place your order through our website's online ordering system, email our sales team, or call us directly. For bulk orders or special requirements, we recommend speaking with our sales team who can provide personalized assistance and quotes.

What is your quality assurance process?

Each piece of plywood undergoes rigorous quality checks at multiple stages. We ensure moisture content, structural integrity, and surface quality meet our high standards. All our products comply with industry regulations and come with appropriate certification.

Do you offer warranty on your products?

Yes, all our plywood products come with a manufacturer's warranty against defects in material and workmanship. The warranty period varies by product type. Please check the specific product details or contact our customer service for more information.
