Shuttering Plywood

Build with confidence using Nice Ply's Shuttering Plywood. Engineered for the rigors of concrete formwork, our shuttering plywood offers exceptional strength, durability, and reusability, ensuring smooth and efficient construction.

Features of Our Shuttering Plywood:

High Strength and Durability

Our shuttering plywood is constructed from hardwood core veneers and bonded with high-strength phenolic resins, providing exceptional load-bearing capacity and resistance to deformation.

Excellent Reusability

Designed to withstand multiple uses in concrete formwork, our shuttering plywood can be reused numerous times, making it a cost-effective choice for construction projects.

Smooth Surface Finish

The smooth surface of our shuttering plywood ensures a fair-faced concrete finish, reducing the need for additional finishing work.

Water and Weather Resistance

Treated to resist moisture and weathering, our shuttering plywood maintains its integrity even in harsh construction environments.

Dimensional Stability

Our shuttering plywood offers excellent dimensional stability, minimizing warping or twisting and ensuring accurate formwork construction.
