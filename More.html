About Us

15 Years of Excellence in the Woodworking Industry

Welcome to SI Industries

Established in 2008, SI Industries has grown to become one of India's leading manufacturers of premium plywood, flush doors, and blockboards. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, combined with rigorous quality control processes, ensure that every product meets international standards of excellence. We serve both residential and commercial sectors, providing innovative solutions for modern construction and interior needs.

Our Mission

To provide superior quality wood products that exceed customer expectations while maintaining environmental responsibility. We strive to be the most trusted name in the plywood industry through continuous innovation and sustainable practices.

Our Vision

To become India's leading wood products manufacturer by 2030, setting new industry standards for quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction while contributing to the nation's economic growth.
